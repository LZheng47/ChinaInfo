China - General Information

    China is one of the most advanced civilizations today. Also known as the People's Republic of China, and has a population of approximately 1.4 billion in 2019. Interestingly, due to China's geographic size, it spans over 5 time zones and yet, China entirely uses one time zone, UTC+8. China's national sport is ping pong. China is home to numerous inventions and discoveries, such as tea, originally used as medicine. There are 8 main dialects, but China's official dialect is Mandarin.

FOOD INFO

The flavors and ingredients of Chinese dishes' flavors vary vastly based on the region of China that it originated from, otherwise comprised of the 8 culinary cuisines. For example, Eastern Chinese food which includes regions such as Guangzhou and Shanghai food, is known for sweet and light flavors, whereas Central Chinese food, consisting of the regions Wuhan and Chongqing, is known for extremely spicy food.
